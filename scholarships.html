THEDREAM.US

TheDream US offers two main scholarships for undocumented students: the National Scholarship, up to $33,000 and the Oppurtunity Scholarship, up to $80,000 for students in states that don't offer in-state tuiton. These scholarships are open to students with or without DACA or TPS. Further eligibilty information is on the website.

Immigrants Rising

Immigrants Rising is an organization whose goal is to transform lives through education. They curated a list of scholarships that don't require proof of U.S citizenship. Users are also able to set parameters to further personalize the list.

Aliento

Aliento is a nonprofit organization. Most recently they were apart of securing in-state tuiton in Arizona for undocumented students. This is also a list of scholarships that don't require proof of U.S citizenship.
