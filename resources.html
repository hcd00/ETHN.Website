ACLU: Know Your Rights When Encourtering Law Enforcement

The ACLU's "Know Your Rights" guide provides vital information for individuals stopped by the police, detailing rights such as the right to remain silent, refuse consent to searches, and request a lawyer. It also offers strategies to reduce risk during encounters and outlines steps to take if rights are violated.
